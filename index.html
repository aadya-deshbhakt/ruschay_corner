Why have I created this website?

A lot of us Lassans/Chirkuts post art, edits, poetry, etc for Ruschay on various social media platforms, right? We do it to show our unwavering love and support to Ruschay! Plus, RuschayVerse is filled with talent! So, why not create our own website? A website to post content for Ruschay to shower them with our love, and also have a platform to display our creativity all to ourselves instead of being dependent on other social media websites? We all know how unstable they can be, with the recent arc of suspension and deletion of fanpages from instagram! The years of love, memories, and efforts of so many people vanished from that platform within a few seconds! But that doesn't mean that our connection can fade away just like that! We will store our edits on this platform, which will be a safe space for Ruschay and all of us! Therefore, we can proudly call this platform our own: "RuschayVerse!"
